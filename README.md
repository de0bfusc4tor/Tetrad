## 𝐅𝐞𝐚𝐭𝐮𝐫𝐞𝐬

- **Aniki Kinetic Engine**: Physics-based sequencing where steps possess mass and momentum, moving through a 4x4 grid via vector navigation.
- **Stage Pulse Engine**: Hardware-inspired 8-step architecture with multi-pulse ratcheting (1-8 pulses per step) and tactile pitch sliders.
- **Stiletto Snake Engine**: A 16-step probabilistic grid designed for evolving, organic patterns that flow in a non-linear snake pattern.
- **Timbre Glide**: Dedicated modulation lanes for continuous parameter control, perfect for filter cutoffs and wavetable morphing.
- **Dynamic Step Logic**: Variable sequence lengths per engine with global swing, gate duration, and clock scaling (1/4 to 1/64).
- **High-Contrast UI**: Brutalist aesthetic designed for low-light environments, featuring vibrant cyan and orange data visualization.

## 𝐒𝐞𝐪𝐮𝐞𝐧𝐜𝐞𝐫𝐬

**Tetrad** provides three distinct environments for melodic and rhythmic exploration:

### 𝟏. 𝐀𝐧𝐢𝐤𝐢
A fluid approach to time where steps are treated as particles with inertia.
- **Kinetic Mass**: Scales step duration; higher mass creates inertia, lower mass creates rapid "flicks."
- **Vector Navigation**: Sequence paths determined by directional vectors (N, NE, E, SE, S, SW, W, NW).
- **Visual Feedback**: Real-time compass and kinetic progress rings.

### 𝟐. 𝐒𝐭𝐚𝐠𝐞
Inspired by the ML185 hardware sequencer, focused on rhythmic density.
- **P:0X Logic**: Multiple triggers per step allow for complex subdivisions and ratchets.
- **Gate Logic**: Individual toggles for rests and ties to create syncopated grooves.

### 𝟑. 𝐒𝐭𝐢𝐥𝐥𝐞𝐭𝐨
An evolving engine for generative rhythmic sequences.
- **Probability Controls**: Integrated chance-based triggers for organic, non-repeating movement.
- **Flow**: The sequence flows through the 16-step grid in a natural, winding progression.

## 𝐂𝐨𝐧𝐭𝐫𝐨𝐥𝐬 & 𝐈𝐧𝐭𝐞𝐠𝐫𝐚𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧
